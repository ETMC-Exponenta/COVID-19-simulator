2. 50% of population stays home

Load recorded data and plot result
load dataStay50.mat
propagationAnimator(data, 'plot');
You see that 50% quarantine doesn't work because all people got virus. A good result is a 20% reduction in peak.
Disease spreading animatoin. Notice the exponential increase in the number of sick people.
propagationAnimator(data, 'play', 'ForceNewWindow', true);

3. 90% of population stays home

Load recorded data and plot result
load dataStay90.mat
propagationAnimator(data, 'plot');
In this case virus infects only 54% of population, and at the peak of the disease we have only 35-40% of sick people.
Disease spreading animatoin. Notice the exponential increase in the number of sick people.
propagationAnimator(data, 'play', 'Ts', 0.3, 'ForceNewWindow', true);
